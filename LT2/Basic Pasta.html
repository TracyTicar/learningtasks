Basic Pasta

Ingredients

  • Dried pasta... any work well
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Tomato pure
  • Cheese/Tuna/Both/None

Methods

  1. Boil your Pasta adding salt to the water for later on.
  2. While pasta is cooking grate your cheese/drain your tuna.
  3. When the pasta is cooked add about half tube of tomato puree, stirring well, to give you a passata consistency. Of course you can just use passata instead.
  4. Then, add the tuna if using any, stirring it well. Leave for a minute to heat up the tuna. add salt/pepper to taste.
  5. Serve into a bowl and sprinkle lots of cheese on top.
