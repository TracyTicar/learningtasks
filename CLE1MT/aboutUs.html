About Us

Tracy established Environmental Destruction in 2015 with the purpose of inspiring people all over the world to open their minds and improve their attitudes and habits in order to live a more sustainable lifestyle.

We believe that major changes necessitate collaboration. We must continue to learn about the ecosystem around us as a global community and instill environmental empathy in our children since that is the only way to reap the endless advantages of nature.

About Environmental Destruction

More than 890 bloggers, teachers, and parents are actively using our online resource to study or teach about environmental concerns such as deforestation, endangered species, trash, pollution, global warming, and many others.
